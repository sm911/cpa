Why 4:1 is the Right Ratio

üéØ Predictability

At 4:1, each core supports a small, controlled number of vCPU time slices. That keeps "waiting in line" (CPU Ready time) low, which users feel as snappy performance.

üìà Headroom for Peaks

Real workloads spike. The 4:1 cap leaves room so short spikes don't starve other VMs.

üè¢ Standards-Aligned

4:1 is a common enterprise planning limit for mixed workloads (OS, apps, moderate DB), balancing utilization and performance.
