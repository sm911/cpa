📎 Strategic Objective

Enable 36-day daily incrementals to remain ≤ 300 TB in IBM Cloud Object Storage (COS) on IC4G/VCF, preserving schedules and RTOs.

Acceptance Criteria (36-day sign-off)

4

Controls

COS Object Lock enforced; access scoped to capacity-tier gateways; logging reviewed

Risk Management

⚠️ Low Dedupe Yield Datasets
Already-compressed/encrypted media
Mitigation: Isolate in separate jobs; enforce exclusions; confirm appliance sizing; tune block size by cohort
⚠️ Restore Rehydration Latency
Older-point restores on inline-dedup repos
Mitigation: Retain recent points longer on XFS; pre-stage Instant Recovery for critical VMs
⚠️ Policy Drift
Risk of Copy to COS or weekly active fulls being enabled
Mitigation: Lock SOBR/job templates; apply change control procedures
